TROUBLESHOOTING:

❌ If you don't see the button:

  1. Make sure you ran: mvn clean compile
  2. Completely close and restart the application
  3. Clear any JavaFX cache
  4. Check the console for errors
  5. Verify you're logged in as ADMIN (not regular user)

✅ The button should be visible immediately after login!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK START:

  1. Run: LAUNCH_AND_TEST_AI.bat
  2. Login with: kiro.admin@nutrilife.com / kiro2026
  3. Look at left sidebar → MANAGEMENT section
  4. Click: 🤖 AI Anomaly Detection
  5. Enjoy the AI-powered dashboard!
